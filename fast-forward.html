Bob Marley Website - Passion Project

BRIEFING

My group and I were tasked during the intro week with a project about thinking ahead and thinking of an invention that could exist in 2050, based on one of the themes given. There were a few different themes like love, sport, infrastructure but my group and I ultimately chose transportation, where we asked the question: “What if we could teleport in 2050?”.
