SITUATION

My group and I were tasked during the intro week with a project about thinking ahead and coming up with an invention that could exist in 2050, based on one of the themes given. There were a few different themes like love, sport, infrastructure, but my group and I ultimately chose transportation, where we asked the question: “What if we could teleport in 2050?”

TASK

Our goal was to develop a concept that was creative but also had some basis in science. We needed to research the idea, design a prototype, and present it.

While the invention didn’t need to be realistic, it had to be backed by some kind of scientific explanation. Our team chose to explore the concept of teleportation, specifically looking into quantum teleportation.

ACTION

We started by researching teleportation, finding articles on quantum teleportation, and creating a mind map to list the pros and cons of the concept. To make sense of the complicated scientific terms, I used ChatGPT to simplify the articles so our team could understand them better.

For the prototype, we were inspired by the Dublin-New York “teleporter” concept. A teammate made a sketch, and I used Lego to build the second version of the prototype, making adjustments to work with the materials we had.

For the final step, I created a presentation using Canva to showcase our project.

RESULT

After seeing other groups' prototypes on presentation day I definitely wish we made something more interactive, as the lego prototype was quite simple. For other future similar projects I'd definitely try and make something more interactive that gives people a real experience.
