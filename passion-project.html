THE TASK

For the passion project, students are allowed to choose whatever project they want. This project is meant to help fill learning outcomes you might be lacking in.

WHY CHOOSE THIS?

During the summer I listened to a ton of Bob Marley, this inspired me to my passion project about him. Aside from listening to his songs all through my childhood, I also just think I'm missing home and his songs remind me of home. I also knew that I could fit many learning outcomes into a project like this which is why I also ended up doing it.

SETTING THINGS UP

I started by collecting images in a pinterest board, trying to understand what design methods convey that gritty feeling you see appear in lots of graphic design for reggae things. I thought it would be fun to incorporate multimedia in this project so I hand drew a few graphics as well as printed some images out and drew above them with markers.

RESEARCHING & PLANNING

I started figuring out the website’s focus and, after exploring Bob Marley content, I found inspiration in a YouTube series called "Legacy". It delves into the lasting impact of his work, which inspired me to create a site about his legacy. I also drew from the graphic design of the series' covers. I outlined the site in a Google Doc, dividing it into five sections and listing the information I wanted to include. Since I wanted to add sound elements, I noted which songs and interview clips to use in each section.

DESIGNING THE WEBSITE

I started designing the first iteration but felt stuck, so I asked Stan for help. I wanted the site to feel soulful and reflect a Caribbean vibe, not just look like a typical website. Stan suggested adding elements like a grain overlay, a paper-like texture, using masking for cutout shapes, and switching to a font with more personality. Applying his feedback to my second iteration made a huge difference, and I loved the result. Many of the layout ideas were my own, which made me proud. I also improved my Figma skills and learned how to create a cohesive vibe for a website.

WHAT WENT WELL

This project went really well because I had a clear idea of what I wanted from the start. Stan's advice and tips were extremely valueble, and I was able to apply the new knowledge I gained to other projects. The overall process was smooth, and the design turned out as I had hoped.

WHAT COULD HAVE GONE BETTER

The main challenge was the time constraints, with less than two weeks for the passion project. Additionally, I was disappointed that I couldn't code my ideas due to both time limits and getting the flu right before the upload date. I spent a lot of time on the design, so next time, I’ll need to work faster to ensure I can complete the entire project.
