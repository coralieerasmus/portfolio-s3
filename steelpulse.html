Steel Pulse Poster - Random (work in progress!)

BRIEFING

One of my favorite reggae bands, Steel Pulse is coming to Eindhoven in May so I decided why not try and make a poster for this event. They're celebrating the 25th anniversary of their most popular album. I started by making a pinterest board filled with photos of the band and also inspiration. This side-project I'm doing is meant to help me learn how to work with photoshop and illustrator since I was just recently able to buy it.

ACTIONS

Looked at inspiration and strated experimenting with a layout. But due to time constraints I havent been able to continue with this. Seeing as this project was meant to help myself learn how to work with photoshop.

APPROACH

For inspiration I looked at other posters of reagge bands aswell as how their branding generally looks like. I started experimenting with a layout in Figma but put a pause to this project because I had my focus on the Go-IT project.

REFLECTION

Definetly wish I had more time to work on this project of mine, since I do really want to learn how to work with photoshop. Hopefully I can finish it before the end of this semester.
